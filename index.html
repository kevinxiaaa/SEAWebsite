Newcomer Youth

YMAP (Leadership and Integration Program)

What is YMAP? YMAP is a program designed to empower students and provide opportunities for leadership, personal growth, and community engagement. Our mission is to support students as they navigate challenges, learn life skills, and build social and professional networks.

Through workshops, career exploration, and volunteer opportunities, YMAP offers students the tools they need to succeed and contribute positively to their communities.
